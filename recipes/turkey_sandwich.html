Turkey Sandwich

Description:

Great lunch. Easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (2 1/2 to 3 pound) skin-on, bone-in split turkey breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Pat the turkey breast with a paper towel to remove any excess liquid and place it into a baking dish just big enough to fit. Use the tip of a sharp small knife to poke the skin of the turkey breast all over. Drizzle with olive oil and use tongs to rub it in; flip the turkey breast over a few times until all surfaces are coated. Set aside for 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. While the turkey rests, mix the salt, pepper, thyme, rosemary, oregano, and paprika together in a small bowl until thoroughly combined.
  4. Use tongs to hold the turkey breast with one hand while using your other hand to sprinkle the spice rub evenly over the entire surface, including the sides. Finish with the skin side facing up.
  5. Add about 1/2 inch of water to the bottom of the baking dish.
  6. Place the prepared turkey breast into the center of the preheated oven. Immediately lower the heat to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Roast until a probe thermometer inserted into the center of the thickest part of the breast reads 150 degrees F (65 degrees C), about 1 hour and 15 minutes.
  7. Let cool before wrapping. Chill thoroughly in the refrigerator, about 8 hours or overnight. Once the meat is cold, slice thinly and serve.
